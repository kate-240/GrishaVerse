    Sun Summoner

    The sun Summoner has the ability to control the light of the sun, they are the only Grisha that has this power, and is known as a saint to the people of Ravka.

    Kefta

    A blue uniform with gold embroidery, then there is the black uniform with gold embroidery.

    Squaller

    The Squaller is able to control the air around them to create wind, storms, sound and sometimes they can create and control lightning

    Kefta

    A squaller's Uniform is blue with silver embroidery around the edges of the uniform.

    Inferni

    Inferni have the ability to control fire as well as flamable gases, Inferni need a fire source or way to make a spark which is why they always carry flint on them.

    Kefta

    Inferni uniform is blue with red embroidery around the edges.

    Tidemakers

    Tidemakers have the ability to control water and are able to turn the water into ice and are able to summon water ftom the moisture from the air around them.

    Kefta

    The uniform the Tidemarkers wear is blue with the pale blue embroidery around the edges

    Shadow Summoner

    The Shadow Summoner has the ability to bend the shadows and control them however they want, the Shadow Summoner is also the is a rare Grisha and their is only two of them.

    Kefta

    The Shadow Summoner uniform is pure black with black embroidery.
