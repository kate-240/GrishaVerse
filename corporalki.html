    Tailor

    Tailors have the ability to change what they look like or the appearance of others, more experienced tailors can make these changes last longer or in some cases permanent.

    Kefta

    Tailors uniform is red with blue embroidery around the edges of the uniform.

    This order is mostly for creating products that help the second army in the war they have with the other countries (go to home page for more information) the order work closely with scientist to create new weapons for the second army as well as creating new defences.

    Heartrender

    Heartrenders have the ability to control a persons body, stop them from breathing, make a person fall into a coma, and crush or stop the person's heart, which makes them one of the most feared Grisha after the Shadow Summoner.

    Kefta

    Healer

    Healers have the ability to heal all types of wounds and broken bones as well which allows the injured person to continue fighting after they have been healed, this makes healer important in the front lines for the second army.

    Kefta
